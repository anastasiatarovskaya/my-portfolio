About me

Designer who turns complexity into clarity

I'm a UI/UX designer equally confident working with complex product systems (dashboards, SaaS platforms) and visual identity for brands and websites.

I care about interfaces that are understood at first glance — no instructions needed. My approach is built on a precise grid, considered typography and solutions without visual noise.

Beyond digital design, I'm interested in composition in physical form — I work with balloon art installations, building balance of shape and colour in space.
